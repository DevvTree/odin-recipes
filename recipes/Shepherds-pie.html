Shepherd's Pie

Making the best Shepherd’s Pie is not difficult. But, there are a few steps to it. You have to make the meat mixture, the mashed potatoes, assemble the casserole, and bake it. I find that the easiest way to make this dish is to work ahead. You could make the beef or lamb mixture ahead of time. Or, you could make the mashed potatoes ahead of time.

You could also make the whole recipe ahead of time. Just assemble the casserole, cover the dish, and keep it in the refrigerator until you are ready to bake it. I find it is best to bake it within 3 days of preparing it. When you are ready to bake it, take it out of the refrigerator for about 20 minutes before you need to bake it to let it start coming to room temperature. Uncover it, and bake.

Ingredients

MEAT FILLING:

  1. 2 Tablespoons olive oil
  2. 1 cup of chopped yellow onion
  3. 1 ib. 90% lean ground beef -or ground lamb
  4. 2 teaspoons dried parsley leaves
  5. 1 teaspoons dried rosemary leaves
  6. 1 teaspoons dried thyme leaves
  7. 1/2 teaspoons salt
  8. 1/2 teaspoons ground black pepper
  9. 1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce
  10. 2 garlic cloves -minced
  11. 2 tablespoon all purpose flour
  12. 2 tablespoons tomato paste
  13. 1 cup of beef broth
  14. 1 cup frozen mixed peas & carrots*
  15. 1/2 cup frozen corn kernels

POTATO TOPPING:

  1. 1 1/2 -2 ib. russet potatoes -about 2 large potatoes peeled and cut into 1 inch cubes
  2. 8 tablespoons unsalted butter -1 stick
  3. 1/3 cup half & half
  4. 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  5. 1/2 teaspoon salt
  6. 1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper
  7. 1/4 cup parmesan cheese

Steps

  • Sauté some chopped onions in olive oil.
  • Add ground beef or ground lamb.
  • Add dried parsley, dried thyme, dried rosemary, salt, and pepper.
  • Stir and cook the meat mixture until the meat is browned.
  • Add minced garlic and Worcestershire sauce.
  • Cook the garlic for a minute.
  • Add flour and tomato paste.
  • Add beef broth, frozen mixed peas and carrots, and frozen corn kernels.
  • Simmer the meaty gravy until it is thick.
  • Boil some peeled and chopped potatoes until they are soft.
  • Drain the potatoes.
  • Add butter, half & half, parmesan cheese, garlic powder, salt, and pepper.
  • Mash the potatoes until smooth.
  • Pour the meat mixture into a casserole dish.
  • Top it with cheesy mashed potatoes.
  • Bake until the meat is bubbling and the potatoes are golden.
